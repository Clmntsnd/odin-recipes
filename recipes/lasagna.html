Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is typically made with layers of pasta sheets, tomato sauce, a rich meat sauce, and plenty of cheese. This hearty and comforting dish is perfect for feeding a crowd or for leftovers to enjoy throughout the week. While it can be a bit time-consuming to make, the end result is well worth the effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flake (optional)
  • 1 container of riccota cheese (15 ounce)
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente, according to package instructions.
  2. While the noodles are cooking, make the meat sauce. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook the ground beef or Italian sausage until browned and cooked through, breaking up any large chunks with a wooden spoon. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the meat and cook until the onion is softened, about 5 minutes. Add the basil, oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using) and cook for another minute, until fragrant. Stir in the marinara sauce and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with 1 cup of the shredded mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup of the grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. To assemble the lasagna, spoon a thin layer of the meat sauce into the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture. Repeat these layers until all of the ingredients are used up, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese over the top of the lasagna.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the edges of the noodles are slightly crispy.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
